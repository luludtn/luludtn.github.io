Un bout d'été en Tunisie 🐚

Tunisia has been making me so curious over the last few years, mainly driven by the people surrounding me. I've always heard about it, got told a million stories from there, could imagine it in some shapes into my head. But it's always been very fictional to me, until the opportunity came. I started photography recently, and fell in love with black and white. One could find it wrong to go to such a colorful place and remove colors. I love it and will love it forever. Now, please discover what I've discovered, look at these faces, these scenes, these moments with these characters. — Un bout d'été en Tunisie, Juillet 2025
